Accounts Receivable Factoring

We enable businesses to improve cash flow by selling accounts receivable from B2B transactions to financial institutions in exchange for early payment.

Network Loan

This is a relationship-based policy finance program that supports SMEs with short-term production funding—up to 80% of the order amount—through partnerships with large corporations, mid-sized enterprises, and high-performing SMEs. It enables SMEs to secure the necessary capital from the early stages of production to fulfill delivery contracts.

Key Features

We provide financial intermediation services to support smooth business funding activities.

  • Deferral of Payment

    • Delaying payment of service charges through loans effectively manage the operation of funds

  • Strengthen long-term relationships

    • Show trust to your business partners helps build long-term relationships

  • Reduce credit risk

    • Receiving funds from financial institutions reduce the risk of delayed payment by ordering companies

  • Early securing of production funds

    • By borrowing the amount to be paid to the ordering company earlyImproving cash flow
