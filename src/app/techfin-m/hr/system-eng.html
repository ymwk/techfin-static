Recruitment Process

Here’s an overview
of the process for joining
Techfin ratings
  1. Document Screening

    We review basic qualifications and core competencies.

  2. Interview

    We assess technical skills and organizational fit.

  3. Offer/Medical Check-up

    Compensation is discussed, followed by a health check.

  4. Reference Check

    For experienced candidates, reference checks are conducted.

  5. Final Acceptance

    Join the team at Techfin ratings!

Recruitment Schedule

  • We are hiring experienced professionals on a rolling basis as part of our business expansion.

Important Notes

  • The recruitment process outlined is standard and may be adjusted
    (steps added, replaced, or removed) depending on the position.
  • Reference checks may be conducted with the applicant’s consent during the hiring process.
  • A pre-employment medical check-up will be required upon final selection.
